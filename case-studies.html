Case Study 01
Microsoft (via partner)

Enterprise Program Communications

Adoption narrative + leadership cascade

Multi-stakeholder, cross-geo program work requiring executive-ready content, tight approvals, and consistent messaging.

Goal

Translate complex enterprise initiatives into clear narratives that drive understanding and adoption
Equip leaders with cascade-ready messaging — briefs, talking points, FAQs
Maintain delivery velocity and quality across overlapping requests and deadlines

Audience

Internal Employees impacted by enterprise initiatives (distributed teams)
Leadership Senior stakeholders needing crisp "say/do" messaging
Program Teams Cross-functional partners coordinating adoption comms

Message Spine

Clarity

"What's changing, why now, and what you need to do."

Executive-ready narratives: leadership updates, briefing notes, FAQs.

Confidence

"Here's the plan, the timeline, and where to find help."

Structured content packs and consistent cadence to reduce ambiguity.

Consistency

"One voice across channels — accurate, inclusive, and repeatable."

Tone-of-voice and inclusive language guidance used as quality criteria.

Assets Shipped

01 · Announcement

Press Note / Internal Announcement

Program update / launch note — what's new, why it matters, next steps.

02 · Social Set

Internal Social / Community Updates

Program headline + "why now" · What's changing + benefit · How to adopt + resource link · Common questions (mini-FAQ) · Reminder + CTA
03 · Event Comms

Webinar / Townhall Invite + Recap

Invite copy + agenda blurb + "what you'll leave with." Post-event recap with key takeaways and next actions.

04 · Exec Comms

Leadership Email + Talking Points

Message hierarchy + timeline. Talking points (5–7 bullets) + FAQ topper for objections.

Results

✓

Delivered consistently in a high-volume environment (~10–20 requests/week) with clear prioritisation and strong follow-through.

✓

Improved communication effectiveness through weekly reporting views in Power BI tracking engagement signals and optimising cadence/priorities.

✓

Strengthened quality and consistency via tone/inclusive-language guidance used across outputs.

Case Study 02
ELMS Sports Foundation

Partnerships + Community Impact Campaign

PR + events + multi-stakeholder comms

Needed donor/partner confidence and community engagement through clear, human-first storytelling.

Goal

Build a cohesive campaign narrative that strengthens partner confidence and boosts engagement
Produce a consistent set of PR + event + digital assets to support partnerships
Create repeatable storytelling workflows for planning → creation → distribution → review

Audience

External Partners, donors, community stakeholders
Internal Leadership and program teams coordinating outreach and events

Message Spine

Impact

"Here's the problem we're solving — and what changed because of this work."

Impact storytelling for donors, partners, and community stakeholders.

Credibility

"Trusted work, real outcomes, clear accountability."

Executive visibility and partnership comms packs supporting stakeholder confidence.

Participation

"Here's how to engage — show up, support, share."

Persona-based campaigns and structured workflows that increased engagement.

Assets Shipped

01 · Announcement

Press Note / Media-Style Update

Campaign announcement + proof points + spokesperson quote.

02 · Social Set

Campaign Social Series (5 posts)

Campaign launch + "why now" · Beneficiary story / human angle · Partner spotlight + credibility cue · Event push + agenda highlights · Thank-you / outcomes + CTA
03 · Event Comms

Invite + Agenda + Post-Event Highlights

Invite copy + RSVP CTA. Agenda blurb with attendee takeaways. Post-event highlights snippet.

04 · Exec Comms

Leadership Note + Spokesperson Talking Points

Leadership note to partners/community. Talking points + mini FAQ for predictable questions.

Results

✓

Delivered end-to-end communications across internal/external audiences; strengthened storytelling for donors, partners, and community stakeholders.

✓

Supported executive visibility and partnership communications through event narratives and publish-ready comms packs.

✓

Increased engagement through persona-based campaigns and structured storytelling workflows.
